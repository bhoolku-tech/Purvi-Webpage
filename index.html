Core Services

Pelvic Pain Management

Addressing chronic pelvic pain, endometriosis-related discomfort, and pain with intimacy through manual therapy and therapeutic exercise.

Pre- & Post-Partum Therapy

Support for pregnancy-related pain, diastasis recti (abdominal separation), and tailored recovery plans after birth.

Urinary Incontinence Treatment

Effective, non-surgical solutions for stress incontinence, urge incontinence, and frequency issues through bladder retraining and muscle strengthening.

Pelvic Muscle Re-education

Using biofeedback and targeted exercises to help patients gain awareness and control over the pelvic floor musculature for optimal function.

Core/Posture Rehabilitation

Integrating pelvic health with the entire kinetic chain to improve posture, resolve lower back pain, and enhance overall core stability.

Pelvic Organ Prolapse Management

Conservative care options, including strengthening, breathing techniques, and guidance on pessary use, to manage symptoms of POP.
